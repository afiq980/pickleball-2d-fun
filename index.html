How to play — scroll to read all tips

🎯
Timing is Everything
Shots made closer to your paddle are stronger and more accurate. Watch the timing bar — green means perfect, red means weak.
🥅
Drive Smart
Drives made when the ball is below the net will always hit the net. Wait for the ball to rise before unleashing a fast drive.
⚡
Split Step Boost
Press Split Step just before your opponent hits the ball to gain a boost in strength and accuracy on your next shot. The button glows gold when the window is open.
💥
Wear Them Down
Every fast drive and sharp dink battle slowly wears down your opponent's accuracy. Keep the pressure on and they'll start making mistakes.
🎮
Click with Intention
Press a shot button only when you want to hit the ball. Spam clicking will result in inaccurate shots and pop-ups that are easy for your opponent to attack.
🔀
Stay Unpredictable
Use different shots to keep your opponent guessing. Repeating the same shot makes it easier for the AI to anticipate and counter your moves.
